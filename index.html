Panache Training & Consulting Pty Ltd has been delivering outstanding training and related consulting services to its customers since being formed by its founder and Managing Director, Lindsay Dewberry, in 2000.


We specialise in the development and delivery of training and assessment materials for vocational training purposes.


Originally specialising in the areas of finance, financial management and the financial services industry, we have expanded to become a valuable and trusted supplier of training across all facets of governance, financial and operational management, including relationship skills development.
