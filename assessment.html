Assessment Services

Most training workshops present the participant with a Certificate of Attendance, but sometimes you want more!


Sometimes you want to know that the learning has "gelled" for you or for others in the organisation. Accredited courses under competency and academic frameworks all have an assessment component. Panache has been developing assessment materials for its clients in the academic and competency arenas since its inception. We are also continuously involved in receiving, "marking" and moderating assessments/assignments completed by participants.


Assessment no longer takes the form of the dreaded classroom exam. It can take many forms including, written, oral, role-playing, practical demonstration etc., as the situation requires. If required, Panache can also develop appropriate forms of assessment for non accredited courses.
