Content Development & Instructional Design

Instructional Design is defined as the process of creating learning or instructional experiences which make the gaining of knowledge, especially by adult learners, efficient, effective and enjoyable. It involves the Training Needs Analysis process mentioned elsewhere and leads to training experiences that are geared to the content needs and the learning styles of the participants. Panache has a strong track record of building meaningful and enjoyable programmes for individuals and organisations. Try us and see!
