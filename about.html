About Our Company

Panache Training & Consulting Pty Ltd has been delivering outstanding service to its customers since being formed by its founder, Lindsay Dewberry, in 2000. It specialises in the development and delivery of business oriented training and assessment materials.


Originally specialising in the areas of finance, financial management and the financial services industry, it has expanded to become a valued and trusted supplier of training across all facets of governance, financial and operational management, including relationship skills development.


Panache delivers its services directly to the public and through third parties.
