About Third Party Connections

Third Party Connections

Lindsay Dewberry holds an Associate membership of the Australian Institute of Management (AAIM) and has been a valued partner as a consultant, facilitator of public and "in company" programmes and author of content for many years.


Over the years, Lindsay has written/updated material for the following AIM courses:

  • Finance For Non Financial Managers,
  • Accounting for Non Accountants,
  • Manage Team Budgets,
  • Introduction to Budgeting
  • Develop a Business Plan
  • Workplace Safety
  • Continuous Improvement

Lindsay regularly facilitates these courses, as well as others, for AIM.


Lindsay is also a CPA level member with CPA Australia. Over the years he has written, edited and facilitated courses for both CPA members and the public in such areas as:

  • Introduction to Budgeting
  • Budgeting For Finance Professionals
  • Accounting For Non Accountants
  • Accounting Essentials
  • Finance for the non financial Executive
  • Understanding Financial Statements

Lindsay also facilitates courses he has created and other courses.

Some of the other training organisations for which he has facilitated over the years include:

  • Akolade
  • ANZIIF (Australia and New Zealand Institute of Insurance & Finance)
  • Australian Institute of Financial Servicesand Accounting Pty Ltd(AIFA)
  • Australia Wide Business Training
  • DeakinPrime
  • Frontline Management Institute
  • ICML (Institute for Communication Management and Leadership)
  • Kaplan Professional
  • Konnect Learning
  • Liquid Learning
  • Northern Group Training
  • PD Training
