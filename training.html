Training Needs Analysis & Consulting

Some clients simply want a course "off the shelf". Others want tailoring as mentioned elsewhere. Panache has a great track record of meeting with clients, developing a Needs Analysis and building the outcomes into a structured plan with the clients, often involving the roll out of an accredited programme or a series of topics that suit the client needs. The Needs Analysis involves a review of current skills of key participants, the skills required for their roles, so that the training programme developed fills the skill gap identified.
