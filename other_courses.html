Other Courses

Non accredited training is training that is typically subject or topic based but does not lead to any recognised qualification.


Many of our clients want dedicated training on particular topics. Non accredited training offers the flexibility of "purpose" built training on a required topic.

  • Finance For Non-Financial Managers

  • Contract Management

  • Budgeting and Performance Measurement
  • Developing and Managing KPI's
  • The Balanced Scorecard and Dashboard Reporting
  • Workplace Safety

  • Implementing Customer Service
  • Managing Customer Service
  • Continuous Improvement
  • Time Management
  • Being a New Supervisor
  • Being a New Manager
