Accredited Courses

Panache is not a Registered Training Organisation (RTO) in its own right. However, through its strategic partnership with Frontline Management Institute (FMI) we can offer our clients access to the following qualifications and focussed programmes:


  • Certificate IV and Diploma Frontline Management*
  • Diploma of Management*
  • Key Management Skill Set
  • Certified Practicing Manager


*These units can be provided with or without access to coaching as part of the service delivery
